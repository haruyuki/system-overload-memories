System Overload Memories plans to be an information hub for everything related to the now shut down System Overload Pixelmon Server. This may be in the form of text, images or videos. The site will contain builds and other content created on or for the server, as well as a short article on the staff members about them and their contributions to the server.


As the server is no longer accessible since its shutdown in mid-2017, this site is mainly intended for the existing players of System Overload at the time who want some nostalgia of some of the old builds and videos created on the server. However, it can be used to serve as an introduction for new users finding the site or knowing about System Overload for the first time to give them a general idea of what the server was like when it was still up and running.


This site will be different from other existing players of System Overload who wants to create a website like this as I was the co-owner of the server since its early days and have many of the original images and assets that normal players would not have access to.
